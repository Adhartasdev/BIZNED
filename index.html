ON BUSINESS IN THE NETHERLANDS?

At BIZNED, we aim to provide you with a set of trust-worthy business trip facilities that you can use in the three largest Dutch cities: Amsterdam, Rotterdam and The Hague. It is here that you will find the top places to conduct business.

Find you way to the most important trade centers in The Netherlands. World trade centers are filled with businesses, hotels and restaurants offering the latest food experiences. We have partner vouchers that can provide you access to some of these facilities. Do not hestitate to request one in the contact form.

Need a flexible working option? Our partners in flexible offices enable you to book your own offices, conference rooms or flex-desk across our designated cities. Choose an option and enjoy a seamless travelling experience.

Looking for a place to stay? We will show you our recommended stays across our key cities. These hotels are a select few that have been chosen for the purpose of improving your trip experience. We intend to provide you with the #top-experience.

Where do you arrive? Our country is small, is neat and is clear. We allow you to see the airports you might arrive in and their facilities. It is important to note that you will probably arrive in Schiphol Airport.

Coming by rails? Find your way from your arrival station and enjoy your seamless BIZNED experience. Discover the stations you will arrive in and their proximity to core business facilities.

Need some advice, some cash or exchange? Find a list of reputable banks and their locations here on BIZNED. Our key city locator will show you where you can go and get your banking needs satisfied. BIZNED, you can bank on us.
